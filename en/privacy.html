Privacy Policy

How we handle your data

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

"What's for Dinner?" (the "Service") collects information for the following purposes:

  • Contact Inquiries: We collect name and email address to respond to partnership and customer inquiries.
  • Service Improvement: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve user experience.

2. Types of Information Collected

  • When Contacting: Name, email address, inquiry content
  • Automatically Collected: Cookies, browsing history, browser information (when using Google Analytics and advertising services)

3. Data Retention

Personal information is deleted promptly after the purpose of collection is achieved. However, if retention is required by relevant laws, data will be stored for the required period.

4. Sharing with Third Parties

We do not share your personal information with third parties except in the following cases:

  • When you have given prior consent
  • When required by law or by lawful requests from law enforcement

5. Cookies

We use cookies to improve user experience and provide personalized services:

  • Theme Settings: Saving dark/light mode preferences
  • Advertising: Google AdSense for personalized ads
  • Analytics: Website usage statistics collection

You can disable cookies through your browser settings.

6. Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to show ads based on your interests.

  • For more information about Google's advertising cookies, please visit Google Advertising Policies.
  • To opt out of personalized ads, visit Google Ads Settings.

7. Image Processing

Images uploaded for the Animal Face Test are processed entirely in your browser and are not transmitted to or stored on our servers. All AI analysis is performed client-side.

8. Contact Us

For privacy-related inquiries, please contact us through our Contact page.

9. Policy Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated due to changes in laws, policies, or security technologies. Changes will be announced on this website.
